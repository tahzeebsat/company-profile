Greetings from the Representative

Valuable products to the world.

We are a company that exports used Japanese cars and construction machinery.

With our basic philosophy of "Valuable products to the world," we have steadily grown our business since the company was founded in 2002, thanks to our carefully selected procurement and unique distribution system.

In 2012, we began custom-made production of cars in China. Since then, we have received support from many customers and have grown into one of our main businesses. In the future,
all of our employees will work hard to become a company that continues to meet the needs of customers around the world, with the goal of establishing an automobile production plant overseas.
Representative Director Malik Saleem
